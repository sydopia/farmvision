Who We Are

Farm Vision Technologies is a startup focusing on development and commercialization of computer vision systems to enable growers of high value crops — such as fruits, berries, and nuts — to monitor the count, size and health of their plants.

We will help growers to make decisions regarding labor and sales far in advance, directly improving their revenue and reducing their costs. The data collection will seamlessly integrate into their normal farm operations. We hope to bring the precision agriculture revolution to fruit production!
